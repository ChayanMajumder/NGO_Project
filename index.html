WELCOME TO PARASH PATHOR

Our Goal is to Help as many people as possible

Parashpathor is a registered Trust, established up in June 2020 at the time of extreme crisis due to Covid 19 pandemic situation with a primary goal to help the distressed neighbours. While initially focusing on the problems of daily earners during pandemic, Parashpathor is currently actively pursuing the development of women, students and the children from disadvantaged families by solving various social issues.
